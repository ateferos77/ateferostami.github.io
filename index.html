About Me

I am a dedicated MSc Bioinformatics student at the University of Padova, Italy, with a strong foundation in cellular and molecular biology. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Cellular and Molecular Biology from Zanjan University, Iran, where I graduated with distinction (17.89/20).

My research interests lie at the intersection of molecular biology and computational methods, focusing on single-cell RNA sequencing analysis, molecular dynamics simulations, and systems biology approaches to understand complex biological phenomena.

Currently based in Padova, Italy, I am actively engaged in cutting-edge research projects and continuously expanding my expertise in bioinformatics tools and methodologies to contribute meaningfully to the scientific community.
